Here’s information about the fee applied to boarding passes for domestic flights collected from an airport counter.
Thank you for being a valued customer of Jeju Air.
We follow due rules and procedures in keeping the personal information you provide us with safe.

A service reform has been effected to provide customers with faster service by cutting down on airport counter wait time.

Before getting to the airport, you can collect a mobile boarding pass, and if you’re at the airport already, you can collect your boarding pass for free from a kiosk. These are the ways to collect your boarding pass quickly and conveniently.

If you have no baggage to check in, head straight to the security check station. If you have baggage to check in, make your way over to a counter with your mobile boarding pass or a boarding pass collected from a kiosk.
Details on checking in for flights can be found on the Jeju Air website.
Information on Transfer of Personal Information to Overseas Entities

Charge: KRW {3,000} per passenger

Where: Airports in Korea ({Gwangju, Muan} excluded)

Applies to: Customers collecting boarding passes from a counter despite the availability of the mobile boarding pass and automated kiosk service

Any of the passengers specified below are able to collect boarding passes free of charge from an airport counter Passengers accompanying an infant, {VIP/GOLD/BIZ LITE/J PASS} passengers, passengers with a booking that requires change (a change fee applies), passengers wishing to book a front-row seat or an emergency exit seat (seat booking fee applies), passengers who requested a service that requires staff assistance (minors traveling alone, pet service, wheelchair service, pregnant women, booking of an emergency exit seat, etc.), passengers who book a flight at an airport (booking fee applies), passengers who received a military discount, passengers who received a personal discount, passengers who booked a flight with a GIFTTICKET, etc.
